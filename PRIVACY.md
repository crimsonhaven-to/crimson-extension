# Privacy Policy — Crimson Haven Companion

**Effective date:** 2 July 2026
**Applies to:** the "Crimson Haven Companion" browser extension (the "Extension").

## Summary

The Extension **does not collect, store, transmit, or sell any personal data.**
There are no accounts, no analytics, no tracking, and no advertising. Everything
the Extension does happens locally in your own browser.

## What we collect

**Nothing.** We (the developers of the Extension) do not receive any information
from the Extension. It does not send data to us or to any server we control — it
has no such server.

## What is stored on your device

The Extension stores a **single setting** locally on your device using the
browser's `storage.local` API:

- Whether the Extension is switched **on or off** (a single true/false value).

This value never leaves your device, is not linked to any identity, and is removed
when you uninstall the Extension. Nothing else — no browsing history, no URLs, no
media data, no personal information — is stored by the Extension.

## What the Extension does with permissions

The Extension requests only the permissions needed to let the crimsonhaven website
play its media sources directly in your browser. In plain terms:

- It adds the network headers that media hosts require and handles cross-origin
  requests so a source can load and play.
- It may briefly open a media source in a background tab in order to start
  playback, then closes that tab.
- Broad site access (`<all_urls>`) is **optional** and is requested only when you
  switch the Extension on. If you never enable it, it is never granted, and the
  Extension does nothing.

All of this processing occurs on your device. None of it is reported to us.

## Third-party services

When you play a source, your browser connects **directly** to the third-party
media hosts and content delivery networks that serve that source — exactly as it
would when loading any web page. As with any normal web request, those third
parties may receive standard request information (such as your IP address and
request headers) and are governed by **their own** privacy policies, which we do
not control. The Extension does not add any tracking of its own to these requests,
and no copy of this traffic is sent to us.

## Data sharing and selling

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade any data, because we do not collect any.

## Children's privacy

The Extension does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including
children.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published at this same
location with a revised effective date.
